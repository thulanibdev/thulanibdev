## 💼 My Companies

<div align="center">

### 〔 01 〕 VELTRIX LOGISTICS
**`CEO & FOUNDER · LIVE & OPERATING · EST. 2026`**

</div>

> **Veltrix Logistics** is a live and actively operating freight brokerage business founded and led by Thulani Denfrod Billy. Veltrix connects shippers who need cargo moved with truckers who have available capacity — negotiating deals, managing loads, and building a trusted logistics network across South Africa. Currently operating manually while the full digital platform is in development.

<div align="center">

| ◆ Service | ◆ Description |
|:---|:---|
| 🚛 Freight Brokerage | Matching shippers with the right truckers across SA |
| 💰 Rate Negotiation | Securing competitive pricing between all parties |
| 📋 Load Coordination | End-to-end shipment and delivery management |
| 🤝 Network Building | Growing a verified network of carriers and clients |
| ⚙️ Digital Platform | Full app & website — currently in development |

</div>

**`FOUNDED: 2026  ·  STATUS: ✅ LIVE & OPERATING  ·  ROLE: CEO & FOUNDER`**

### 〔 02 〕 CONNECTIQ
**`FOUNDER · STUDENT NETWORKING PLATFORM · BUILDING`**

</div>

> **ConnectIQ** is a student-first networking and community platform — the LinkedIn meets Discord built exclusively for students. While LinkedIn serves professionals and Discord serves gamers, ConnectIQ fills the untapped gap: a premium dedicated space where students across South Africa can connect, collaborate, discover opportunities, and build their futures — together.

<div align="center">

| ◆ Feature | ◆ Description |
|:---|:---|
| 🎓 Student Profiles | Academic-focused profiles built for students not executives |
| 🤝 Peer Networking | Connect with students across every SA university & college |
| 💬 Community Spaces | Subject & interest-based group chats and communities |
| 💼 Opportunities Board | Internships, bursaries & jobs curated for students |
| 📅 Campus Events | Discover events happening across campuses nationwide |
| 🧠 Study Collaboration | Find study partners and groups across institutions |

</div>

**`STATUS: 🔨 BUILDING  ·  VISION: The home of every SA student  ·  ROLE: FOUNDER`**

<div align="center">

### 〔 03 〕 STUDYFLOW
**`FOUNDER · EDTECH STARTUP · BUILDING MVP`**

</div>

> **StudyFlow** is an all-in-one student productivity platform that replaces the chaos of juggling five different apps. One clean, intelligent workspace — built by a student, for every student — that handles scheduling, tasks, notes, timers, and progress tracking all in one place.

<div align="center">

| ◆ Feature | ◆ Description |
|:---|:---|
| 📅 Smart Scheduling | Automated timetable builder around your subjects |
| ⏱️ Study Timer | Pomodoro-based focus sessions with break reminders |
| ✅ Task Manager | Per-subject task lists with priorities and deadlines |
| 📝 Note Organiser | Structured, searchable notes per subject |
| 📊 Progress Tracker | Visual study stats, streaks, and weekly reports |
| 🔔 Deadline Alerts | Exam countdowns and submission reminders |

</div>

**`STATUS: 🔨 BUILDING MVP  ·  LAUNCH: 2027  ·  ROLE: FOUNDER & LEAD DEV`**
